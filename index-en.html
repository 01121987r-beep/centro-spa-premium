About

A beauty retreat conceived as an urban refuge, refined yet deeply genuine.

Aura Botanica was created to offer a different pace: softened interiors, curated protocols, precise manual techniques and treatments selected for both aesthetic and sensory wellbeing.

Every experience is shaped with attention to detail, natural materials and a calm, professional and deeply relaxing approach.

Signature services

Quiet wellbeing, natural materials and thoughtful gestures.

Our idea of luxury is not excess, but felt quality: generous space, tactile textures, botanical scents, soft light and rituals centred on listening and care.
